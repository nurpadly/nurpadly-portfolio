ABOUT ME

I'm Nur Padly, a professional 2D Background Artist with over 10 years of experience in the game and animation industry — based in Indonesia, working with clients worldwide.

My specialty is the world behind the characters. I build environments that ground a story, set the mood, and make players and viewers feel like they've stepped into another world entirely.

Every scene I deliver comes fully layered in Photoshop — organized, labeled, and production-ready for your pipeline. No cleanup needed on your end.

I've spent years working within studio pipelines for both game and animation production. Now I bring that same discipline and craft to freelance clients globally.

Workflow

HOW I WORK

01
Brief & Direction

We align on scope, style references, mood, color palette, and technical specs before a single stroke is made.

02
Sketch & Comp

B&W compositions and value studies are shared for approval before moving to full color rendering.

03
Color & Render

Full painterly rendering with milestone check-ins built in. Feedback is incorporated early — never at the end.

04
Final Delivery

Layered PSD/PSB source files + transparent PNGs, organized and labeled for immediate production use.
